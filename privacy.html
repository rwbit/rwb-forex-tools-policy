This Privacy Policy explains how RWB IT ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with the RWB Forex Tools mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect any personal information from you. The App does not require user accounts, logins, or any input of personal details such as name, email, location, or financial data. Since we do not collect any information, there is no usage of data. The App's functionality is entirely offline and self-contained.

This Policy complies with applicable privacy laws, including but not limited to GDPR (for EU users) and CCPA (for California residents), to the extent relevant. Since no data is collected, there are no data subject rights requests to process, but you may contact us for any concerns.
